BeoSound 5c

Version 1.0.0 | Build 2025.01

A modern recreation of the iconic Bang & Olufsen BeoSound 5, bringing the classic circular arc interface to life with contemporary technology. Integrates seamlessly with Sonos, Spotify, and Home Assistant for complete home entertainment control.

Built on Raspberry Pi 5, featuring the original B&O hardware input devices including the laser pointer, navigation wheel, and tactile buttons.
